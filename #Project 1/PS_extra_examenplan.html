Exams

Exams are compulsory and determine whether you pass a class and get your diploma or not. An exam can take place anytime during your study program. An exam plan is an aid in compiling and developing a reliable and relevant study.

Exam plan

An exam plan describes what the exams of the study program you're taking will look like. Down below are things that you will find in an exam plan.
1. Which exams you will take during your education, where and when.
2. What kind of exams you can expect (practical, theory).
3. When you pass the exam ( for example, if you atleast obtain a 5.5).

If you want to know more about your exam plan, contact your study career coach or go to the techniek college info point.
