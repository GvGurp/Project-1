What is canvas?

Canvas is a course management system that supports online learning and teaching.Post grades, assignments and information can be posted by professors.And students can view them through canvas. Canvas is being used by colleges and universities all around the world. Do you want to go to the canvas login page?
Click here

Courses

In canvas courses you can see all your subjects. If you click on one of those subjects, you will see more about that subject. You can then see your learning goals per lesson and week in an extensive planning. You can also see your grades, assignments and feedback per subject.

Calender

In the canvas calendar you can see your schedule. You can see all your courses next to the schedule with a color. Each course automatically has a color that you can see in the calendar. It is possible to change the color of a course in the agenda.You can also choose whether you only want to see one week of your schedule or if you want to see a whole month.

Inbox

In the canvas inbox you can see emails sent by your teachers. You can also send an email to one of your teachers. You choose per course to whom you want to send the email.
