Read more about it down below

Program structure

The software developer course has a duration of 3 to 4 years. One year consists of 4 periods. In year 1 you learn every period a new scripting language, programming language or an information system. In each period you also have an exam of your other subjects such as English, math and so on.

BPV period

BPV stands for practical training.At a recognized training company (internship or working and learning) and at an accredited workplace you gain practical experience during your MBO/HBO course.
In year 2 and 3 of the software developer course you will do an internship at a company.
