Guidance

At techniek college rotterdam receives every student guidance.We are talking about guidance from teachers, the study career coach and the internship coach. But sometimes students need more guidance. This could be because of their home situation, because they feel down/gloomy, because they need extra structure, because they have doubts about their studies or just because they need a little extra attention.

Guidance team

Techniek college rotterdam has a team for students that need more guidance.The team is made up out of specialists who can help you with every course. We call this the team support.

You can ask for extra guidance through your study career advisor or through the techniek college info point.
